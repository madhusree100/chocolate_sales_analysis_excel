<h2><a class="anchor" id="overview"></a>Overview</h2>

This project evaluates the chocolate sales  performance by addressing High-Performing countries and chocolates. A complete  vizualization and analysis was achieved using Excel.

---
<h2><a class="anchor" id="business-problem"></a>Business Problem</h2>

The chocolate company wants to understand sales trends over three years, identify top performing products , analyze regional sales distribution , Track shipping performance and detect seasonal patterns. With the above knowledge the company wants to build a plan to improve product focus, optimize regional marketing strategy and to provide data driven decision.
